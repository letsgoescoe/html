Dominique Escoe

Dominique Escoe was born and raised in Detroit, MI. Heavily influenced by a friend of the family Detroit native singer Aaliyah Houghton. Dominique grew an interest in performing arts. Her mother enrolled her into the School of Performing Arts, Spain Middle School. Dominique joined the modern dance team as well as the majorette team. Evolving into a great stage performer, Dominique was active in two Detroit All City dance competitions, and danced for the NAACP freedom dinner in Detroit, MI in 1999 with thousands of guest including President Bill Clinton. Later during high school, she was chosen as the “2003” Denby Dancer of the year and elected as Co-Captain. With a strong background in dance, Dominique decided to embark into other aspects of the industry by auditioning for roles in music videos and movies. She has been in Cross Over 2006 Preston Whitmore as a cheerleader, Another Heist 2006 Sean Sport Coat as Linda Parker, and Sparkle 2012.
